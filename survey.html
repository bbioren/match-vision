Before you start

  • You'll be asked to allow camera access and complete a quick 9-dot calibration.
  • Then you'll watch 5 soccer clips (about 40 seconds each). Just watch naturally.
  • We record where on the video your eyes look — coordinates only, never video of you.
  • Sit roughly arm's length from the screen with your face well lit, and try to keep your head still.
Privacy: All face processing happens on your device via WebGazer. We only save anonymous gaze coordinates (where you looked on each clip) and timing — never images or video from your camera.
