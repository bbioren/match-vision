How it works

  1. Grant camera access when prompted
  2. TensorFlow.js + MediaPipe FaceMesh detects iris landmarks in real time
  3. Three Y signals are blended: lid-gap ratio, iris Z depth, and head pitch
  4. Eye position maps to a zoom + pan on the video — no calibration needed
